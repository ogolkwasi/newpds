About The Event

CONFERENCE

The conference, known as "barefoot networking," echoes the ethos of Polkadot Safari, focusing on connecting attendees with nature. Held at a venue nestled within a wildlife conservancy, attendees can expect encounters with friendly zebras, giraffes, and other wildlife, offering a distinctive and exhilarating backdrop for showcasing African talent and featuring renowned speakers.

BOOTCAMP AND HACKATHON

The Bootckathon Program offers hands-on workshops for attendees to explore the latest trends in Polkadot blockchain technology. Participants will apply new skills to their projects, fostering cross-functional problem-solving abilities. They'll engage with mentors, fostering diverse perspectives and innovative solutions for Africa's challenges. The longer-term hackathon, alongside in-person events, promises excitement and collaboration. Overall, the program equips attendees with valuable knowledge and skills to drive impact in their fields.
